Our Partners

At CareerCrafters, we believe that partnerships are key to tackling unemployment in our country. By collaborating with innovative companies like Yoco, Luno, and Yebo Fresh, we have been able to provide our users with access to valuable resources, job opportunities, and skills development programs. These partnerships have not only helped us to better understand the needs of the job market but have also enabled us to create more effective solutions to address the challenges of unemployment. We are committed to continuing to build strong partnerships with like-minded organizations to further our mission and make a meaningful impact in our community.
